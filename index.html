About Wayne Intend Pools

Your Trusted Pool Specialists

With years of experience in the pool construction and renovation industry, Wayne Intend Pools has established itself as a leader in creating breathtaking aquatic spaces. We combine technical expertise with artistic vision to transform ordinary backyards into extraordinary retreats.

Our team of certified professionals is dedicated to delivering exceptional quality, from initial design to final construction. We understand that a pool is more than just a water feature, it's the centerpiece of family memories, relaxation, and entertainment.

Our Mission

To create exceptional swimming environments that enhance lifestyles through innovative design, superior craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred pool construction company in the Western Cape, recognized for transforming outdoor spaces into luxurious, functional, and sustainable aquatic retreats.
